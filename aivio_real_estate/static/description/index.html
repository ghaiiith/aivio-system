AIVIO Real Estate Management

A clean Odoo 19 Community real estate module for managing projects, buildings, units, reservations, ownership and rental contracts, installment schedules, and customer invoices.

Projects & Regions

Organize real estate inventory by regions, projects, buildings, floors, and units with company-safe records.

Unit Lifecycle

Track availability, reservations, sold/rented units, maintenance, pricing, images, amenities, and linked products.

Contracts & Invoices

Create ownership or rental contracts, generate installment plans, and create customer invoices from installments.
